About Us

Pa Somjit’s Khanom Jeen Shop is a local Thai rice noodle restaurant that has been serving customers for over 20 years. Located in an old community area, the shop has a warm and friendly atmosphere. Its highlight is the freshly made Khanom Jeen noodles prepared daily and served with a variety of traditional curry sauces, such as spicy jungle curry, coconut milk curry, and northern-style Nam Ngiao. All recipes are original and have been passed down from Pa Somjit’s mother. The shop also offers a wide selection of fresh vegetables and side dishes, making it a popular destination among both locals and tourists who come to enjoy authentic Thai flavors.
