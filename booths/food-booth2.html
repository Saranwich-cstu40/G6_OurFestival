Singha

(Singha Corporation)

About Us

Singha Corporation is the commercial arm that manages the famous Singha beer brand and other beverage lines. The brewery family began in 1933 and today Singha is brewed in Thailand and sold worldwide.

Singha beer

Singha is a full-bodied pale lager (≈5% ABV) brewed from 100% barley malt, selected European hops and Thai spring water. A classic, slightly hoppy premium Thai lager that pairs well with spicy and grilled foods. Try it when you want a bold, authentic Thai beer experience.
