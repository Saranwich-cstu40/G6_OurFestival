The White Hair Cut

Hue Pacharapon Wongsaroje, THE WHITE HAIR CUT is a one-man indie pop band from genie records, known for its unique style and catchy melodies.

History

The White Hair Cut is a one-man band under Genie Records. His journey began in high school, driven by the dream of becoming an artist. He learned multiple instruments and learned music production on his own, allowing him to create his work independently—songwriting and arranging to mixing. This hands-on approach gives his music a unique identity and a clear sense of self-expression.
