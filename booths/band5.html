History

From university classmates to Thai indie-pop sensations, Pemika "Cake" and Songmuang "Muang" bring a unique, heartfelt charm to their music. Starting with covers on YouTube, they quickly gained a following for their soft, melodic sound. Their debut single "ไม่อยากนอน" (Wanna Sleep) became a viral hit, cementing their place in the indie scene.

They continue to release music that resonates with the new generation, focusing on honest lyrics and catchy, dream-pop melodies.
