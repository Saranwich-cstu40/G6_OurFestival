Oppa deak

(FOOD ME CO.)

Oppad Deok (sometimes transliterated as Oppa Tteok) is a popular Korean restaurant chain in Thailand that specializes in Tteokbokki (spicy rice cakes) and other Korean street food. Known for offering a variety of tteokbokki styles and toppings, the restaurant provides a casual dining experience, often catering to groups with shared sets and buffets to enjoy a wide range of K-Food staples. With branches in various locations, including Bangkok (like Ladprao) and Pathum Thani (such as near Thammasat University Rangsit), Oppad Deok is a go-to spot for Thai diners seeking authentic and customizable Korean comfort food.
