เขียนไขและวานิช

เขียนไขและวานิช is a one-man folk song band from Chiang Mai run by Jo Saroj, the man behind all the work he did every thing himself.

History

The name “เขียนไขและวานิช” comes from a printmaking technique studied by the artist during university, meaning roughly “wax & varnish print” , a metaphor for blending contrasting elements to create something authentic and unexpected. Though the name sounds like a duo, it’s in fact the solo project of Jo Saroj Yodying, who writes, plays guitar and sings under this moniker.Their music often features gentle guitar, warm vocals and reflective lyrics about love, nature and life’s quiet moments.
