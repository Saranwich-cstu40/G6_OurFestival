History

Tilly Birds is a Thai pop band formed in Bangkok, consisting of Third (Anuroth Ketlekha), Billy (Thuwanon Tantiwattanaworakul), and Milky (Thitiphat Arthachinda). The band started as a group of high school friends who shared a love for music and began uploading their songs online. The name “Tilly Birds” comes from Third’s nickname “Tilly” and the word “Birds,” symbolizing freedom and creativity. Their unique style blends pop, rock, and R&B sounds with emotional lyrics that connect deeply with listeners.

They signed with Gene Lab, a label under GMM Grammy, and rose to fame with their 2020 hit single “Same Page?” (คิด(แต่ไม่)ถึง), which became one of Thailand’s most popular songs. Following that success, they released other well-loved tracks like “Just Being Friendly” (เพื่อนเล่น ไม่เล่นเพื่อน) and “Can’t Keep Up” (ลู่วิ่ง). Today, Tilly Birds is recognized as one of Thailand’s leading modern bands, known for their creativity, heartfelt storytelling, and strong connection with fans.
