Whal & Dolph

In late 2017, the beloved independent label What The Duck welcomed new artists Whal & Dolph, who brought easy-listening music full of unique sounds and a relaxed and approachable character, earning them a loyal following even as independent artists.

History

Por and Namwon met while in college. They were in an alternative rock band together before. They have known each other for 6 years and because they have similar lifestyles, love playing music, and besides working in the same place, our houses are close to each other. They decided to make music together. They chose this name because they felt that the two animals seemed like friends, kind animals like them, not sharks that look fierce and ferocious.

When talking about whales, people think of Dolphin. Por was the lead singer of The Jogging Boy and Fits, while Namwon was the guitarist for The Public Mansion. Namwon invited Por to do a side project and was also persuaded by Ball Scrubb to join What the Duck. They debuted with their first single in 2016.
