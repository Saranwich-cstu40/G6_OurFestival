YENTED

YENTED is a Thai pop band from Phayao, formed in 2018. Their music blends various genres. These genres are influenced by the members' current interests, so it's no surprise that you can't predict their musical direction.

History

YENTED is a Thai pop band from Phayao Province. They debuted in 2018 and currently consist of four members: Jao-Pongthon Pametho (lead vocals), Toon-Phanukorn Mala (guitar), Kan-Thanakan Suwannakin (guitar), and Bew-Wuttichai Khuenwichai (drums). They are signed to notbad! Music, a subsidiary of Brite Panther, a music entertainment company. Their musical journey began when Jao, Toon, and Mos, then second-year students at the University of Phayao, formed a band and released acoustic pop songs on YouTube. The trio then decided to add four more members to their band to compete in a major music competition.

Their music is predominantly pop, but also incorporates a variety of genres, including rhythm and blues, neo-soul, and hip-hop. Their musical direction is unpredictable, often told through the stories of unrequited love, which fill entire playlists. Their breakout single, "Her," featuring Araryozi and Chcolate – t, tells a brighter love story with a clear hip-hop element and surprises listeners with Japanese rap.
