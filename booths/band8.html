Meyou

MEYOU, whose real name is Chisanucha Tantimeth, is a Thai singer, songwriter, and producer. She entered the industry by winning the "MBO THE AUDITION" contest.

History

MEYOU or Mew Chisanucha Tantimeth, a handsome and charming young man with many talents, including singing, dancing, acting, and the winner of the "MBO THE AUDITION New Faces Ready to Be Born" contest, has worked in various fields in the industry and is well known with many fans. MEYOU was born on January 7, 2000. She entered the music industry through the "MBO THE AUDITION New Faces, Ready to Be Born" project of MBO, a subsidiary of GMM GRAMMY.
