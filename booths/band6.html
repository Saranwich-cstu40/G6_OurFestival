No One Else

"We do everything from the start writing, composing, arranging, producing, and even mastering that's why we called it No One Else."

History

No One Else is a unique musical band and vocal in Soul R&B style, formed by vocalist - Kitiwat Seangprathep( Mhai ), Keyboardist - Jittipol thavorn( Kook ) and Guitarist - Jirutt tantivaraangul( Natty ) the band wad started by Kook and Natty, they studied in Thammasat University together , after graduated they've playing for Lipta band while doing their own music but still missing a vocalist until they met Mhai and released their first single "ยังไม่ลืม" in 2018 which was a big hit

Their name "No One Else" came from their working behavior that they do everything by themselves from the start of idea until releasing.
