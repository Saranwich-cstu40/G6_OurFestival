BIG ASS

Big Ass is a Thai rock band under Genie Records. A veteran rock band with a long musical career and many hit songs that everyone knows.

History

Big Ass began with Aphichat Promraksa (Moo) and Poonsak Chaturabun (Aof), high school students at Satriwitthaya 2 School. They wanted to form a band to play music as teenagers, but at the time, it was purely for fun. After graduating from high school, they each went their separate ways to pursue their own careers.

Aof later enrolled at Dusit Construction College, where he invited his schoolmate Ekkarat Wongchalad (Ek or Dax). Meanwhile, Moo, enrolled at the College of Fine Arts, invited his college friend Ekkarat Rattanapintha (Ton) and his younger brother Khajondet Promraksa (Laem or Kob) to join the band, which was called "Big Ass."
